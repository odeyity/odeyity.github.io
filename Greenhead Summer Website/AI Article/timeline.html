Timeline

2012

AlexNet releases and uses GPUs for training neural networks, heavily decreasing error rate and proving neural networks as the definite way forward

2011-16

Virtual assistants are developed and included in everyday devices such as Siri (Apple), Cortana (Microsoft) and Assistant (Google)

2020

GPT-3 is unveiled by OpenAI, a new startup company focused on AI development, that could generate human-like text and code with significantly more capacity than other similar models.

2022

ChatGPT is launched to the public using GPT-3.5, noted for its human-like nature, however also misleading information
